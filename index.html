About Us

Get to know us

A western modern concept café. Wild Flour Café (WFC)- Founded in 2022. It all started from the idea of being another place that serves pastries as well as western food due to the hype of pastries nowadays.


Mission

Provide guests with excellent, reasonably priced food and incredibly friendly service for their returning satisfaction and enjoyment. In order to meet our customers' ever-evolving tastes, WFC works hard to offer high-quality, reasonably priced goods. Our commitment is to consistently develop novel ideas and broaden our menu to delight our patrons. Thus, everyone will have a memorable and pleasurable dining experience, we periodically assess and modify our services.


Vision

Being a top brand in Cyberjaya is the goal of WFC. Using closely controlled quality and product standards, we are able to spread our love and passion for gourmet food and affordable services to every state.
